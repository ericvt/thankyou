This wine comes from a tiny region in the center of france famous for its wine and food called Sancerre.

My home town is about 50 miles away from there and while the center of france is relatively flat and mostly covered in forest, this area combines hills and flats. It's also located right on the longest river in France.
The geography and moderate climate of this area make it a good place for winemaking.

Romans cultivated the area in the first centuries AD, perhaps already wine.
